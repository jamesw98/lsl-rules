Lightspeed Saber

Scoring & Penalties Reference

Definitions

En-route
Attack has started & blade is ≤ 90° arc
Direct
Hit landed without touching opponent's blade during the hit

Scoring

0
No Points Awarded
Simultaneous
Both fencers hit at roughly the same time.
Ambiguous
Both fencers acted, but the exchange was too messy for the referee to determine what happened.
1
1 Point
Indirect
I hit you, but as I hit you I made contact with your saber's blade.
First Contact
I hit you, but you also hit me. My blade made contact first.
Headshot Override
You hit me below the knee first, but I hit your head at the same time or second — en-route.
3
3 Points
Clean Contact
I hit you. You didn't hit me.
Intercept
I made a direct hit to you first, and you hit me, but your attack was not en-route.
Headshot
I made a direct hit to your head first, or cleanly.
5
5 Points
Return
You attacked me. I made a defensive blade action (parry, beat, deflector, shield, etc.) against the attack, then followed up with a direct hit.
Pre-return
I attacked you. In response, you attacked me, which I then defend with a successful defensive blade action.

Penalties

Minor
Threat
Any action that threatens an illegal action or presents danger. Examples: point in line, rising attacks below the waist, holding, excessive power that does not hit.
Power
Excessive force that makes contact but does not cause injury. Example: a hit to the hand that pushes through because of late retraction.
Hazard
Illegal contact that was accidental and does not cause injury. Examples: unintentional stab to the hand, grazing with the tip during a cut.
Major
Repeated Power
Given when a fencer receives a second, third, or fourth Power call in the same match.
Menace
Any action that is unsafe, meant to intimidate an opponent, or is deliberately brutal.
